Consent Form

Please do not participate if you are a minor.

You will participate in a short demographic questionnaire, followed by a cognitive science experiment.

Be aware that the experiment takes about 10 minutes.

Try to do it as a single block, alone, in a quiet place, staying focused.

Your cooperation is essential for the data to be interpretable. Your participation is free, and you can stop at any time, by leaving this page.

However, if you stop before the end, the data will not be usable and no data will be kept.

Only your responses and response times will be analyzed and stored. The results of this experiment may be used in publications or scientific presentations. There are no risks or discomforts associated with this study other than those of daily life.
